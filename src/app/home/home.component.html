  • 1.A.Short name, extension and commencement.—(1) These Rule may be called the Bihar Motor Vehicle (Amendment) Rule 2019
  • B. It shall extend to the whole of State of Bihar.
  • C. It shall come into force from the date of its final publication in the Bihar Gazette
  • 2.Rule 64 of Bihar Motor Vehicle Rule, 1992 shall be substituted by following :- ‘‘64 (1) The registering authority shall allot to the vehicle a registration mark in series which shall begin with the registration mark allotted to the State under sub-Section (6) of Section 41 of the Act and shall be followed by State Code number assigned to the registering authority and then by the figure (alphabet), if any, allotted by the State Transport Commissioner, but the registering authority shall not start series in new alphabet until he had obtained prior written permission of the State Transport Commissioner.’’
  • 2.2.The registering authority, on receipt of an application for registration of a motor vehicle, shall assign registration mark under sub-section (3) of State Rules in order of receipt of applications.
